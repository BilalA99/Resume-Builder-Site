My Brand Logo

This logo is a visual representation of what my Resume Builder project stands for. I designed it myself, starting with a hand-drawn sketch of a tilted briefcase mid-swing, with papers slipping out the top. That motion reflects the feeling of being on the move — chasing opportunities, applying for jobs, and taking steps toward a better future. The briefcase isn't just a bag, it's a symbol of preparation and ambition, and the flying papers represent the resumes and experiences people carry with them on that journey. I kept the style clean and minimal on purpose, because that's exactly what I wanted my site to be — something that makes building a resume feel less overwhelming and more doable. This logo is simple, but it means a lot to me. It reminds me of how this project started and where I hope it can go.
